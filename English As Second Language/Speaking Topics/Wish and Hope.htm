Wish & Hope

说明希望

A: Tomorrow I want to go to Boston for the day.

B: Oh, good. Good idea.

A: I'm going out to get a hot dog at the fast food restaurant. Can I get you anything?

B: Will you buy me seven donuts.?

A:  Sure.

A: What time do you want to leave?

B: Anytime is OK with me, but if a night flight is available, let's take that. It's much cheaper.

A: Would you like to sleep in back?

B: No, I would prefer to see the sights..

A: Would you like to go into the details now or after lunch?

B: Well, perhaps we could do it now.

A: Don't go for a moment. I'd like your advice.

B:   What's the problem?

A: Hope the food is worth the drive.

B: You won't be disappointed.

A: How long will you be in the United States?

B: I want to stay here for three years, but it depends on my finances.
