Way and Procedure

询问方法与手续

A: Do you know how to fasten your seat belt?

B: No.

A:  I'll help you.

A: How are we getting to the airport?

B: Why don't we take a taxi, OK?

A: How do I go about insuring this?

B: Please go to counter number 7.

A: What procedure must I go through to join the association?

B: Please fill out this application form first.

A: Want a demonstration now?

B: Could you?

A:  At your service.

A: How can I get downtown?

B: Take bus number 39 and get off at 73rd Street.

A: Are you through with your boarding procedure?

B: Sure. I finished it an hour ago.

A: How do I telephone Tokyo?

B: You can dial the number direct.
